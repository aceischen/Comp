Beyond Reach

Sam Fuller
Beyond Reach

Beyond Reach explores the unconscious, those below-the-surface fears, fantasies, and anxieties only dimly accessible to the waking mind. For this series, I have chosen to work with large format film. The relatively slow process of analog image making—creating sketches, setting up shots, hand developing and scanning each negative—requires me to linger within these dark dreams. The moody abstraction of the black and white tonal palate, combined with the hyperreal detail provided by the use of large negatives, allows me to summon up and, for a time, inhabit these dark visions.

Production Resources

Below are document templates to aid in the preparation phases of your production.

Click the previews on the left to download each template as an excel (.xlsx) file.

Shot List Template

Shot lists are what the director of photography and the director create to understand what they will be shooting during production. Create them with your desired edit in mind. Follow a logical progression through the script, and keep the shots organized based on the order of events of the script. The Assistant Director will organize the shots based on when they will happen over the course of production.


Schedule Template

The schedule is a direct translation of the shot list for the assistant director to have on set to understand what shots are happening and when. The Assistant Director will take the shot list that has been made and organize all of the shots in a logical progression of how the set should run to complete each scene in a timely manner. This should be in depth enough that the Assistant Director never needs to look at anything else other than the script throughout the process.


Call Sheet Template

These are used to notify your cast and crew of important information before your shoot. Included on the sheets are descriptions of times, locations and scenes involved for each day of shooting. Make sure that you fill them out in their entirety to make sure that there are no questions your cast and crew may have prior to shooting.
